FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do drivers need special hardware or a GPS device?
+
No. SmartVan uses the driver's existing Android smartphone. There is no hardware to purchase or install. The driver simply downloads the SmartVan Driver app, logs in, and starts their route.
How accurate is the live tracking?
+
Location updates happen every 5 seconds using the device's native GPS (accurate to 3–5 metres in open areas). In dense urban areas, accuracy is typically within 10–15 metres. The ETA engine accounts for traffic data via Google Maps API.
Is my child's data safe and private?
+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Only verified parents can see their own children's location. SmartVan does not sell or share personal data with any third parties. We comply with Pakistan's PDPA and Gulf PDPL regulations.
Can we use SmartVan in Arabic?
+
Yes. The parent app, driver app, and admin panel all support full Arabic RTL (right-to-left) layout. Gulf customers in Saudi Arabia, UAE, and Qatar can switch the entire interface to Arabic with one setting.
What payment methods are accepted?
+
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) via Stripe. Bank transfer (IBFT) is available for Pakistani customers. Enterprise clients in the Gulf can pay via wire transfer in SAR, AED, or QAR.
How long does setup take?
+
Most schools are fully live within 48 hours of registration. After account activation (within 1 business day), a typical school with 10 vans and 200 students takes about 2–3 hours of admin panel setup. Our onboarding team assists every step of the way.
Can we cancel or change our plan?
+
Yes. Plans can be upgraded, downgraded, or cancelled at any time from your admin panel. Annual subscribers receive a pro-rated refund on unused months if they cancel early (within 30 days of purchase).

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Who We Are

SmartVan is a product of i9Experts Technology, headquartered in Lahore, Pakistan. We provide school transport management software serving schools, transport companies, parents, and drivers in Pakistan and the Gulf region.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration: school name, admin name, email, phone number, and billing details. From the mobile apps we collect:

  • Driver GPS location (only during active trips)
  • Student pickup/drop-off timestamps
  • Push notification tokens
  • Device identifiers (for authentication)

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the SmartVan service: showing live van locations to parents, logging attendance, sending alerts, and generating reports. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 on our MongoDB Atlas infrastructure. Access is restricted to authorised personnel only.

5. Children's Privacy

SmartVan does not directly collect data from children. Student records are managed by the school administrator and linked to parent accounts. Parents may request deletion of their child's data at any time.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days. Trip location data is retained for 90 days. You may request full data deletion by visiting smartvanride.com/delete-account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Requests can be submitted to: privacy@smartvanride.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Regulatory Compliance

SmartVan complies with Pakistan's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and the Gulf's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) applicable in UAE, KSA, and Qatar.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@smartvanride.com

Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. SmartVan's website (smartvanride.com) uses a minimal set of cookies to function correctly and improve your experience.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Authentication session tokens, CSRF protection. These cannot be disabled as the platform requires them to function.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous page view data via a self-hosted analytics tool. No data is shared with Google or Meta.
  • Preference cookies: Your language preference and billing currency selection.

Mobile Apps

The SmartVan parent and driver apps do not use browser cookies. They use secure device-level storage for authentication tokens.

Managing Cookies

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Clearing essential cookies will require you to log in again. Disabling analytics cookies does not affect the platform's functionality.
